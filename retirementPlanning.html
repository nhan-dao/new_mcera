Retirement Planning

Wherever you are in your career, it's never too early to plan for your future.

This part of the MCERA website focuses on the issues important to you before your retirement--whether you are newly hired, in the midst of your career, or late in your career. At any of these stages you can be thinking about what comes next. Your own retirement is an integral part of the plan.


MCERA provides you with a defined benefit plan. Once you have reached retirement eligibility and apply for retirement, your future lifetime benefit is determined by a formula that is based on three factors:

  1. your age at retirement,
  2. your retirement-compensable salary, and
  3. your total service credit.

The various pages on this site will help you understand this formula, each of the three factors, and much more.
